Grummm: posts, projects and runtime demos

Grummm publishes editorial posts and runtime-ready projects inside one modular product. The site is structured so each post and each project can be discovered, read, and revisited through direct routes instead of being trapped inside a single client-side screen.

Visitors can open articles that explain architecture and product decisions, then move into project pages and runtime demos that show how those ideas are implemented. This gives the site a stronger crawl surface, clearer internal linking, and separate pages for separate topics.
