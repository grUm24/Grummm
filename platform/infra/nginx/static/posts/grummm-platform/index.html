Grummm Platform

Editorial overview of the modular monolith, showcase layer, and admin workspace.

Published on March 16, 2026

Grummm combines a public showcase, a secure admin area, and runtime-ready modules in one platform. The public side focuses on editorial posts and portfolio presentation, while the admin workspace controls projects, templates, and deployment flows.

Each module keeps its own contracts and infrastructure. That makes it possible to evolve analytics, platform operations, or business features without rewriting the core.
